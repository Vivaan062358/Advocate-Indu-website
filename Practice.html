Our Practice Areas

Family Law

Divorce, custody, alimony, domestic violence, maintenance.

With over 12 years of experience in family courts, Advocate Indu Rani has helped over 500 clients resolve sensitive family matters through both litigation and mediation. We focus on protecting the interests of children and ensuring your legal rights are upheld.

Criminal Defense

Bail, FIR quashing, IPC, NDPS, POCSO, appeals and defense.

We provide aggressive defense in criminal cases including false FIRs, dowry harassment, and bail applications. With 300+ criminal hearings handled, our strategic approach has led to numerous acquittals and favorable bail orders.

Civil Litigation

Contract breach, recovery suits, property partitions, injunctions.

Our civil practice includes dispute resolution through trial and pre-trial settlements. We ensure thorough documentation and persuasive argumentation. We have successfully represented over 250 civil litigants in Haridwar & Dehradun courts.

Property & Revenue Law

Mutation, title disputes, lease, registry, land partition.

Advocate Indu Rani specializes in title verification, succession, partition and demarcation issues in SDM & Tehsildar courts. We provide complete documentation support including registry, stamp duty and mutation services.

Legal Drafting

Agreements, wills, POA, affidavits, gift deeds, sale deeds.

All legal drafts are prepared with precision and legality. From family arrangements to business contracts, our documents ensure enforceability and clarity. We also assist with notary & registration procedures.

Writs & Appeals

High Court writs, criminal/civil revisions, second appeals.

Advocate Indu Rani has filed several successful writ petitions under Article 226 and criminal revisions in the Uttarakhand High Court. Appeals are handled with deep legal research and client-focused strategies.

Consumer & Medical Law

Medical negligence, poor services, builder disputes.

We represent clients in Consumer Forums for grievances related to housing, insurance, and hospitals. Our approach includes fast evidence compilation and out-of-court settlements wherever possible.

Mediation & Arbitration

Private dispute resolution for civil, family, and business matters.

We provide mediation support to reduce court time and cost. Over 60% of our clients have benefited from early resolution through arbitration or Lok Adalat proceedings.
